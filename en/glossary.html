Glossary

Paleontology
Science that studies fossils to understand the history of life on Earth.
Fossil
Remains or trace of a living organism preserved in sedimentary rocks.
Ichthyosaur
Prehistoric marine reptile resembling a dolphin, having lived during the Mesozoic era.
Plesiosaur
Marine reptile with a long neck and flippers, contemporary of the dinosaurs.
Pterodactyl
Prehistoric flying reptile, often mistakenly called "flying dinosaur".
Jurassic
Geological period extending from -201 to -145 million years, part of the Mesozoic.
Lyme Regis
Coastal town in England famous for its fossils and Mary Anning's workplace.
